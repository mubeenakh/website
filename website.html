Experience Kerala - ponmudi


Located about 61kms away from Thiruvananthapuram, Ponmudi is a small picturesque hill station in Kerala. Pleasant climate, superlative landscape with ravines, rivers, mountains and forests to accompany, Ponmudi proves to be one of the best hill stations in Kerala. At Ponmudi, you can enjoy trekking and hiking, especially at the Agasthyarkoodam, which is the highest peak of the district. The view of the sprawling tea gardens mesmerizes every traveller here.
