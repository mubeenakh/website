ERNAKULAM - KERALA TOURISM

Ernakulam is a district of Kerala, India, that takes its name from the eponymous city division in Kochi. It is situated in the central part of the state, spans an area of about 3,000 square kilometres, and is home to over 9% of Kerala population. Its headquarters are located at Kakkana

MARINE DRIVE


Marine Drive is a buzzing waterfront district known for the Marine Walkway, popular for evening strolls, and leafy Subhash Bose Park. The modern Rainbow and Kettuvallam bridges offer backwater views, and cruises leave from jetties by the water. Affluent homes and offices fill the area, and shops along Broadway sell jewelry, spices, and local fabrics.

Cherai beach is the ideal place to watch the sunset while you are in Cochin. About 40 mins drive from the city area this beach is very long with many beach restaurants and shops nearby.
